  • 20 April 2021

    2:00pm-3:00pm INTERCULTURAL RELATIONSHIPS

    Navigating academic intercultural relationships requires intercultural fluency. This workshop introduces participants to the fundamentals of effective intercultural communication to support them in fostering productive relationships in the academic environment, with a special focus on the transition from a student-supervisor relationship to an academic peer relationship.


    Facilitated: Dr. Sanja Ivanov, Educational Developer, ITA Programs, Centre for Teaching and Learning

    This workshop is offered by the Own Your Future doctoral professional development program. Master's students and postdocs are welcome to attend.
